Add to Home Screen

Follow these steps in Chrome

1Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots) in the top-right corner of Chrome
2Tap "Add to Home screen"
3Tap "Add" to confirm
4Memory Lane will appear on your home screen like an app ✦

Using Samsung Internet? Tap ☰ → "Add page to" → "Home screen"

Follow these steps in Safari

1Tap the Share button (square with arrow) at the bottom of Safari
2Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
3Tap "Add" in the top-right corner
4Memory Lane will appear on your home screen like an app ✦
